The content of this .zip file is a design for: "PanzaBoard V1.1", an
interface between a Raspberry Pi-Pico microcontroller, and a set of
motors and sensors that are suited for educational robotics projects.

This work was financed by University of Southern Denmark's UAS
section under the label: RoboTronix.

The intended use is education of students attending the Robotics
education as well as other relevant students at University of Southern
Denmark (SDU), under supervision by SDU.
